Jeff Bezos is an American entrepreneur and the founder of Amazon.com. He played a pivotal role in the growth of e-commerce and is one of the wealthiest people in the world.


Born on January 12, 1964, in Albuquerque, New Mexico, Bezos had a vision of creating an online marketplace that would revolutionize the way people shop. In 1994, he founded Amazon in his garage, starting as an online bookstore and later expanding to sell a wide range of products.


Bezos' leadership and innovation have made Amazon a global giant in the technology and retail industries. His impact on the business world and technology landscape is profound.
